Debt Management

Understand your debt and learn how to pay it off faster.

What is Debt?

Debt is money you owe to lenders such as credit cards, loans, or other financial institutions.

How Interest Works

Interest is the cost of borrowing money. The higher the interest rate, the more you will pay over time.

Tips to Pay Off Debt

Focus on high-interest debt first, make consistent payments, and avoid taking on new debt.
